# Beta version for FMI Layered Standard for Structured Data (FMI-LS-STRUCT) v1.0.0-beta.1 published

The FMI Project is happy to announce the beta pre-release v1.0.0-beta.1 of the FMI Layered Standard for Structured Data (FMI-LS-STRUCT) on top of the FMI 3.0 Standard.

It is focused on the definition of sampled maps:
- Maps sampled on rectilinear grids
- Maps sampled on irregular grids

Compared to v1.0.0-alpha.1 many smaller improvements have been made, and the incomplete and inconsistent support for Modelica CombiTables has been removed. (CombiTables support is still planned for future versions of FMI-LS-STRUCT.)

Thanks to the FMI Project team and especially Klaus Schuch (AVL)!

Learn more here: https://github.com/modelica/fmi-ls-struct/releases/tag/v1.0.0-beta.1
